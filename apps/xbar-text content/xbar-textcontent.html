Control Chart Detective

Study an X-bar control chart, decide whether a process is behaving like its historical pattern, and separate control limits from specification limits. This app is designed for undergraduate operations students learning process control.

Chart Area

Center line: Historical average sample mean.
UCL / LCL: Expected range for sample means if the process stays in control.
Red points: Warning signs that something unusual may be happening.
Student task: Inspect the chart first. Then answer the questions on the right. Look for points outside limits, long runs on one side of the center line, drift, or sudden jumps.

Decision Check

Answer all four questions, then press Check My Answers.
