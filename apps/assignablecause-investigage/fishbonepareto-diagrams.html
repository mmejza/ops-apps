SPC Root Cause Lab

Use a fishbone diagram to organize possible assignable causes and a Pareto diagram to decide where to investigate first. This interactive app is built for undergraduate students learning statistical process control.

Investigation Workspace

Fishbone = organize possible causes
Pareto = prioritize biggest categories
Goal = investigate assignable causes

Part A. Build the fishbone diagram

Step 1: click a cause from the bank, then click the category where it belongs. Causes you have already placed stay visible but change appearance slightly.

Part B. Use the Pareto idea

Step 2: rank the cause categories from highest to lowest frequency based on the defect data collected by the team.
